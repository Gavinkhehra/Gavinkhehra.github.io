LeBron Kills The Nuggets

On March, 7th, 2018. LeBron James aka"THE GOAT" had an amazing game against the Denver Nuggets dropping an amazing 39 points, 10 assists and 8 rebounds

This is a really remarkable game considering LeBron James is 33 years old and this is his 15th season in the NBA, this game is also special because he got to verse his former teammate Richard Jefferson. This game gave LeBron James his
39th double double of the season.

Real Madrid vs PSG

The game started intense wiht both teams not letting anyone score for the first half with both teams missing all of their shots. Then the second half began and not even 10 minutes after the half whistle the main man of both teams, Christiano Ronaldo scores in the 51' minute then Edison Cavani ties it up in the 71' minute after that Casemiro gets the final laugh with his goal in the 80' minute for Real Madrid.
